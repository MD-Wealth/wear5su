// WHAT IS GOSU?

THE 고수
PHILOSOPHY

Gosu (고수) is a Korean term used to refer to a highly skilled person.

In computer gaming the term is used to refer to a person who dominated games like StarCraft or Counter-Strike — a player so skilled they operate on a different level.

Figuratively meaning pro or highly skilled at something, Gosu's pre-computing usage usually referred to Martial Arts or the game of Go.

The 5SU trademark stands for expressing your obsession with Taekwon-Do, Gaming and Life. Wear what you are. Become Gosu.
